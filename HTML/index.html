This application takes your academic history, and asseses your probable success
in select classes based on the cumulative past preformance of other students.
The classes selected are common for freshmen and across many majors. It is
merely a decision support tool, and is not meant to replace one-on-one advisement
with an experienced faculty member. Nonetheless, use and feedback are appreciated.
This application is in it's experimental stage and will be revised as more is learned
and more data is aquired.
